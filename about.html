About PUMA

PUMA (President University Movie Assignment) is a creative multimedia project developed by Group 1 – MIT 1 – Batch 25 as part of our Web Programming assignment under the guidance of our lecturer Mam Oca. This project combines cinema, storytelling, and modern web development into a single interactive platform. Through PUMA, we aim to showcase our technical skills, teamwork, and passion for visual storytelling by transforming a regular university task into an engaging digital experience.
